Our Story✨

Where Tradition Meets Luxury

A Legacy of Grandeur

Located in the heart of Hyderabad, ASR Convention & Resort was built with a single vision: to provide a majestic space where life's biggest moments can be celebrated without compromise.

Whether it is a grand wedding for 3,000 guests or an intimate corporate retreat, our 30,000 sq. ft. pillar-less hall and lush resort greenery offer the perfect backdrop. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail, world-class hospitality, and a commitment to making every event legendary.

Our Mission

To redefine the event experience by combining vast space with personalized luxury service.

Our Vision

To be Hyderabad's most preferred destination for grand celebrations and peaceful resort stays.
